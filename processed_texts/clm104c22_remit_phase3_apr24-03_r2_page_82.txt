70.7- HH PPS Partial Episode Payment (PEP) Adjustment 
(Rev.) 
A-02-103 
Medicare systems apply two codes to the ERA to indicate a PEP adjustment is being 
reported.  The codes are defined as follows: 
B20 - Payment adjusted because procedure/service was partially or fully furnished 
by another provider; and 
N120 - Payment is subject to home health prospective payment system partial 
episode payment adjustment.  Beneficiary transferred or was 
discharged/readmitted during payment episode. 
These are not applicable to the standard paper remittance advice.
