GROUP THERAPY
• 97158 is intended to be reported for QHP-led group sessions only. 
• When performing direction of technician the code used is 97155 and it must be 1:1 per client, per 
technician with other qualifications of the code descriptor which will then qualify for concurrent 
billing according to the intent of the codes.
• A group includes at least 2 patients but no more than 8. 
• 97154 for technician-led group sessions is reported for each patient attending a group session. 
• Each member of the group can have different funding sources (eg specific health plans or private 
pay).
