occurred prior to the 31st day after initiating therapy. Refer to Local Coverage 
Determination L33718.

3B REASONABLE USEFUL LIFETIME (RUL) 
CP400 
The medical record documentation does not support there was an in-person evaluation 
by their treating practitioner prior to replacement of PAP device following the 5-year 
reasonable useful lifetime (RUL).  Refer to Medicare Program Integrity Manual 5.9, 
Local Coverage Determination L33718 & Policy Article A55426. 
CP401 
The medical record documentation does not support the beneficiary continues to use 
and benefit from the PAP device following the 5-year reasonable useful lifetime 
(RUL). Refer to National Coverage Determination 240.4 & Local Coverage 
Determination L33718.
